Lasagna Recipe

Freshly made lasagna cooked from the below recipe

Prepare this easy lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked for when you need it during a busy week. Then just bake for an extra 45mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • Tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • A little grated nutmeg
  • 300g pack of fresh lasagna sheets
  • White Sauce
  • 125g Mozzarella torn into thin strips
